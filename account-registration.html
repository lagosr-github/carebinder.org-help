• Select the "Register" button from the navigation menu on the left hand side of the screen.

 

 

 

 

 

 

  • Fill in the registration form
     

 

  1. Enter your email address
  2. Enter a password for your account
  3. Re-enter the same password
  4. Click on the reCaptcha checkbox to verify that you are not a robot
  5. Click on the REGISTER button

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

  • An email verification message will be sent to your email inbox, you must verify your address before using CareBinder.org

 

 

  1. The destination address will be shown on the dialog
  2. If the email is lost or never arrived, you may re-send the message by clicking on the "RESEND CONFIRMATION EMAIL" button.

 

 

 

 

  • When you receive the confirmation message, there will be a link that you must visit to complete the verification process.
  1. Visit the LINK in the email to verify your account
  2. You might need to copy and paste the URL into a browser window.

 

 

  • Once you you have verified your email, a dialog window will appear to let you know you can start using your CareBinder.org account.

 

  1. Click on the LOGIN button to log in to your acount.
